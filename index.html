Why RM

How RM brings about inspiration

RM, best known as leader of BST, is one of legendary icons in the music industry. Aside from being part of the award winning songs like Dynamite, Permission to Dance and Butter, he has also made a very successful solo debut with his latest album Indigo.
He is known to be a genius lyricist and is known to be the youngest most-credited song writer in Korea. His songs are known to convery deep and meaningful messages.
Not only does he plays a big part in the songwriting and music production of BTS, he is also a well admired spokesperson and representative for his home country. He has inspired many and gained praise for his speeches at the UN but also the White House, conveying messages of love and inclusion.
Both through his music and messages, RM truly is an inspiration to many.
